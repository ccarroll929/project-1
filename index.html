About:

This web application allows users to find fitness exercises for a specific muscle/muscle group and also calculate the total number of calories they consume each day. Simply enter a muscle/muscle group into the Enter Muscle Group text field and click Get Exercises. Then you can enter a meal in the Meal entry box and then click the Add Meal button to add it to the list and the number of calories will be displayed! Continue adding meal items and the total number of calories in all meal items will be calculated.
